Help & Support

Welcome to the City Events Hub Help page. Here’s how you can use the portal:

  • 📅 Visit the Register section to sign up for upcoming events.
  • 🖼️ Browse past event photos in the Media section.
  • 📍 Use the Locate feature to find nearby events based on your location.
  • 📝 If you have questions, reach out to us through the community office.
